Finished Goods Traceability Demo

Demonstration of order and shipment management, mapping traceability of every lot (batch) using blockchain - through OpenDSU technology.

The web applications above (which should be easily compiled to native mobile Android/iOS applications) are just for demonstration purposes, and will not exist in the future (so, no real effort was done on usability with large data volumes). The long-term vision is that every company (MAH, Wholesaler, Pharmacy) already has their own ERPs, and integration with the blockchain should be provided by Webservice gateways that translate the existing order/shipment management ERP operations on to blockchain read/write operations (so that each company operates as usual as they already do today).
